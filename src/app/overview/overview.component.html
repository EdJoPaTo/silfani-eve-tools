A collection of tools to use with the great game EVE Online made by the player behind the ingame character Rell Silfani.

Pilot Analyzer

Ever wanted to know about the combat strength of pilots in a chat channel or the local?
Just copy them into the Pilot Analyzer.

try it

Item Estimator

You got some items in your cargo and want to know reliable how much that stuff is worth?
A contract? cargo scanner? Just select them and copy (Ctrl + C) them from ingame into the item estimator!
Now you just know how much your items are worth in a Market Hub or Region.

try it

Security Agent Missions

With the use of EVE-Survival you can prepare what kinds of enemies you have to face within an Security Mission.
For german players there was a great list of german - english translated missionnames from Darkblad, but it was discontinued.
I asked for the data and hosted it again with an own UI.

try it

Survey Scanner

You ever wondered about the survey scanner not displaying the volume of the astroids?
Wanted to know how many cycles you can enjoy the silence?
This tool will give you a quick overview of your current belt.
